Veronica O'Hara Yoga


Teaching Yoga since 2008

Veronica found her passion for yoga after the birth of her third child. She casually attended a yoga class at a local gym and left feeling nothing short of elation. She found herself attending more classes and was invited by a friend to attend a new yoga studio in Lighthouse Point and it was a Bikram Yoga Studio. The love of yet another form of yoga was set in place and soon the teachers at the studio were encouraging her to get trained and certified. Bikram training was too far and too expensive so she sought out alternate methods. Veronica found Barkan Method for Hot Yoga in Spring of 2008 and did Level I with Jimmy Barkan in Fort Lauderdale June of 2008. In October of 2008 she completed Barkan Method Level II/III and began her teaching career.

Veronica teaches a careful blend of yoga methods and styles to create a full body experience encompassing alignment, core, creative sequencing that makes sense, careful instruction to assure safety during practice, provides guidance through Yoga’s 8 limbs all set to a groovy blend of music selections. Veronica believes that Yoga is a healing modality both physically and mentally and she believes that Yoga is a Path to Peace and encourages her students to visit their mat often whether it’s in the studio or in their own home.
